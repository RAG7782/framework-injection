## 1. Definition by Genus and Specific Difference

### 1.1 Digital Craftsmanship (Artesanato Digital)

**Proximate genus:** Epistemic praxis for human-AI interaction.

**Specific difference:** Distinguished from other epistemic praxes by:
1. Semiotic foundation (prompt as sign, agent as algorithmic interpretant)
2. Ethics as first constitutional layer (not reactive guardrail)
3. Integrated adversarial cycle (CAAJ 2.0)
4. Full Language (Linguagem Plena) as communicative standard

**Formal definition:**

> Digital Craftsmanship is an interdisciplinary epistemic praxis for the creation, transfer, and governance of artificial intelligence, founded on semiotics (Peirce), linguistic typology (Greenberg), and learning theory (Vygotsky/Sweller), governed by ethics as first constitutional layer, and validated by adversarial refutation.

**What it is NOT:**
- Not a methodology (though it contains methodologies) — methodology is techne; DC is praxis
- Not a philosophy (though philosophically grounded) — philosophy contemplates; DC acts
- Not a doctrine (though it has dogmas) — doctrine is static; DC incorporates self-refutation
- Not a framework (though it contains frameworks) — framework is structure; DC is structure + foundation + ethics + practice + reflection

**Essential properties** (without which it ceases to be DC):
- Semiotic foundation
- Ethics as C1
- CAAJ adversarial cycle
- Linguagem Plena

**Accidental properties** (may vary without destroying essence):
- Number of operational layers (currently 7)
- Specific domains of application
- Specific implementation (AGORA is one implementation, not the only possible one)

### 1.2 Framework Injection

**Proximate genus:** Cognitive transfer mechanism between human and AI agent.

**Specific difference:** Distinguished from other transfer mechanisms by:
1. Completeness — transfers entire reasoning method, not fragments
2. Internalization — agent adopts the pattern, not follows instructions
3. Natural language encoding — no code, no training, no fine-tuning
4. Domain specificity — each framework is artisanally crafted

**Formal definition:**

> Framework Injection is the epistemic mechanism of unidirectional transfer of complete domain-specific reasoning methods — including decision hierarchies, evaluative criteria, ethical boundaries, and output standards — from a human expert to an AI agent, encoded in natural language and operating as a persistent cognitive substrate that the agent internalizes and applies, distinct from instruction (which prescribes actions) and contextualization (which provides information).

**What it is NOT:**
- Not a technique (though usable as one) — technique is procedure; FI is mechanism
- Not a method (though it has steps) — method is implementation; FI is the underlying principle
- Not a paradigm (though it participates in one) — paradigm is worldview; FI is operational mechanism within a paradigm
- Not a concept (though it was conceptualized) — concept is mental abstraction; FI is real process with measurable effects

**Essential properties:**
- Transfer of complete reasoning method
- Internalization by the agent
- Natural language encoding
- Domain specificity

**Accidental properties:**
- Taxonomy size (currently 5 types)
- Specific theoretical connections
- Execution platform

## 2. The Relationship: Constitutive Inherence

The relationship between DC and FI is not subordination, complementation, or dependency. It is **constitutive inherence** — FI is constitutive of DC as the engine is constitutive of the automobile.

| Relationship tested | Verdict | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Subordination (FI subordinate to DC) | Partially true but insufficient | FI can exist independently of DC — someone can do FI without semiotics, without CAAJ, without Linguagem Plena |
| Complementation (DC and FI complement each other) | False | Complementation implies mutual independence; DC without FI loses its most distinctive feature |
| Dependency (DC depends on FI) | True in one direction | DC depends on FI for operational distinctiveness; without FI, DC is "philosophical prompting" |
| **Constitutive inherence** | **Most precise** | FI is the constitutive mechanism that makes DC operationally distinct; DC is the epistemic context that makes FI theoretically grounded |

## 4. The Lakatosian Research Program

The entire ecosystem constitutes a Scientific Research Program (Lakatos, 1978):

**Hard core** (irrefutable assumptions defining the program):
- Intelligence is craft, not factory
- Human expertise is semantically transferable to AI
- Governance must be constitutional, not reactive

**Protective belt** (modifiable auxiliary hypotheses):
- Specific taxonomies (5 FI types, 7 AGORA layers)
- Specific metrics (IFS, TFI, Pidgin Gap)
- Specific implementations

**Positive heuristic** (development rules):
- Extend to new domains
- Formalize theoretical connections
- Validate empirically
- Publish with DOI

**Negative heuristic** (protection rules):
- Never reduce to prompt engineering
- Never abandon ethics as C1
- Never accept output without adversarial refutation
